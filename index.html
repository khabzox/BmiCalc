What is body mass index?

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure used to estimate whether a person is a healthy weight for their height. This index helps assess the risk of developing certain weight-related diseases.

How to interpret the results:

  • Less than 18.5: underweight
  • 18.5 - 24.9: healthy weight
  • 25 - 29.9: weight gain
  • 30 and above: obesity

Remember, BMI is a general guide only. Consult your doctor for a comprehensive health evaluation.
